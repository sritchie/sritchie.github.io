Storehaus provides a unified asynchronous interface to key-value stores in Scala, backed by Twitter Futures. It includes composable store combinators and ready-made adapters for Memcached, Redis, MySQL, and more. Storehaus powers the real-time merge layer in Summingbird, enabling online serving of aggregated results.
