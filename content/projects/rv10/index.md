An ongoing build of a Van's RV-10, a four-seat, all-metal, low-wing airplane kit. The RV-10 has a cruise speed of ~200 mph and a range of ~1,000 miles.

The airplane will be registered as N720AK. The [N720AK POH](https://github.com/sritchie/N720AK) documents the aircraft's pilot operating handbook.
