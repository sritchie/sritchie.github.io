FORMA (Forest Monitoring for Action) is a system for detecting tropical deforestation in near-real-time using MODIS satellite data. Built on Cascalog, FORMA processes terabytes of satellite imagery to produce monthly deforestation alerts across the global tropics. Developed at the World Resources Institute with support from the Center for Global Development and the Norwegian Government's REDD+ initiative.
