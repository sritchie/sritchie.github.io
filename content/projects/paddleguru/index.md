PaddleGuru (later expanded into [RaceHub](https://www.racehubhq.com/)) is a race registration and results management platform for paddlesports and other endurance events. The platform handles online registration, live results, and athlete profiles for hundreds of races. Built from scratch as a full-stack Clojure/ClojureScript application.
